Biography

Hello! I’m Mario, your favorite mustachioed plumber, and the star of the Mushroom Kingdom! You might know me from my adventures jumping on Goombas, tossing Koopa shells, and saving Princess Peach from that big, mean Bowser (seriously, he needs a new hobby). By day, I’m a plumbing expert—fixing pipes, unclogging drains, and keeping the Kingdom’s waterworks flowing. But when adventure calls, I grab my trusty red cap and become the ultimate hero. With my brother Luigi (he’s-a number two, but we don’t-a talk about that too much), we leap into action, smashing blocks, collecting coins, and powering up with mushrooms and fire flowers.
